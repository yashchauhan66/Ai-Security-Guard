# DPDP Readiness

CyberRakshak Guard supports DPDP readiness workflows for AI guard logs and related SaaS operations. This is not legal advice or a compliance certification.

Readiness controls:

- Data subject request tracking.
- Data export and deletion request tracking.
- Consent evidence records.
- Data processing records.
- Retention policy linkage.
- Privacy incident workflow.
- Breach-notification draft workflow.
- Organization audit logs.

Destructive deletion must require confirmation, authorization, and audit logging.
